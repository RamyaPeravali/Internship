Flexi Personal Loans are just like Personal Loans, but only smaller and more convenient. Flexi Personal Loans are small-ticket size loans that usually start from ₹1,000 up to ₹10,000, with short repayment tenures. Since Personal Loans seldom come with small ticket-sizes (less than ₹50,000), Flexi Personal Loans are ideal to take care of small expenses or unexpected emergencies. They can also act as an infusion of much-needed funds in a cash crunch at the end of the month. So, whether it’s a utility bill/insurance premium payment you need to make, or last-minute gifting for somebody’s birthday, a Flexi Personal Loan will help you with the necessary funds if you’re running short of cash.

Frequently Asked Questions.

What is the repayment tenure offered for the Flexi Personal Loan Product?
You need to repay your Flexi Personal Loan anywhere between 2 months (62 days) to 6 months from the Loan Disbursal Date depending on your Loan terms.
How long does it take to disburse the amount?
As long as you are eligible for a Flexi Personal Loan and your profile is confirmed, you can get your Flexi Personal Loan amount transferred directly into your bank account within just 15 minutes of application.
My application for a Flexi Personal Loan did not get approved. Why?
Your application may not be approved due to multiple reasons such as a duplicate profile or low Credit Score. If you feel it is in error, you can drop us a mail we will rectify any errors.
What is the Minimum Loan Amount I can apply for as my first Flexi Personal Loan?
You can apply for a minimum amount of ₹1000 up to a maximum of ₹ 2 Lakhs. The minimum loan amount you are eligible for depends upon factors such as your credit score, repayment history, and other data points.
Can I re-apply for a Flexi Personal Loan in case I couldn't avail it at my first attempt?
Sure, you can. You can re-apply for a Flexi Personal Loan within 3 months of your last application. However, the approval of the same will happen solely at the discretion of Company.
