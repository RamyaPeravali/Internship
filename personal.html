Frequently Asked Questions.

What is the repayment tenure offered for Personal Loan for Salaried?
The repayment tenure for Personal Loan for Salaried range from 3 months to 15 months.
How long does it take to disburse the amount?
As long as you are eligible for a Personal Loan for Salaried, you can get your Loan amount transferred directly into your bank account within just 1 working day of application.
Is there a minimum salary amount that is required to avail Personal Loan for Salaried?
Yes. Your monthly net salary should be greater than ₹15,000. However, it is not necessary that everyone with a salary above ₹15,000 will be given the option to avail a Personal Loan for Salaried, since there are other parameters also that need to be accounted for.
Can anyone get a loan of an amount bigger than their salary?
Yes. You can avail Personal Loan for Salaried up to a maximum amount of 200% of your monthly net salary.
How should employees account for the variable payment component(if any) while entering their monthly salary details?
They can take an average indicative of the incentive amount and add it over to their fixed income. However, their Personal Loan for Salaried will only be approved if it reflects in their salary statement.
Can employees take a loan if they are serving their notice period in their current company?
Since the Personal Loan for Salaried is being provided to the employee against their current salary statement, we prefer providing loans to employees who stick to their current company during the tenure of the loan..
Can employees who don't have a company-provided ID apply for a Personal Loan for Salaried?
Yes, of course. As long as they have valid salary slips and can validate their salary details (either through Net Banking or by uploading their bank statements in PDF format), they can avail a Personal Loan for Salaried from us.
